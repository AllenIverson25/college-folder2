Discover USF

A premier public research university dedicated to student success, innovative research, and community engagement.

Leading the Way in Higher Education

The University of South Florida is a high-impact global research university dedicated to student success and positioned for membership in the Association of American Universities.

Founded in 1956, USF has rapidly ascended to become one of the fastest-rising universities in the nation. Our commitment to excellence spans across all academic disciplines, from groundbreaking medical research to innovative engineering solutions.

Our Mission

To generate, transmit, and apply knowledge to promote human understanding and improve the quality of life. Through our commitment to truth and excellence in teaching, research, and service, we honor our responsibility to strengthen the economic, human, and social capital of the communities we serve.

Financial Aid FAQs

To apply for financial aid at USF, complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) at fafsa.gov. Use USF's federal school code: 001537. Priority deadline is March 1st for the best aid consideration.

USF offers merit-based scholarships, need-based grants, and special talent awards. Automatic consideration for many scholarships occurs when you apply for admission. Additional scholarships are available through individual colleges and departments.

Yes! USF offers Federal Work-Study positions, on-campus employment opportunities, and career services to help you find part-time work that fits your academic schedule.

Financial aid can be applied to housing and meal plans. We also offer payment plans to spread costs over the semester and various housing options to fit different budgets.
